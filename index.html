Goals are powerful and universal. They're the fuel for all of humanity’s great accomplishments, and helping people achieve their most outlandish goals is our highest calling. We now have the technology and understanding to help people achieve them faster and with fewer resources. I’m starting a company that will do just that through a combination of machine learning (AI), concierge-like services and a lot of hard work.

This is a big, nebulous goal. There’s a lot of mud to crawl through to get there. But it will be worth it (and a hell of a lot of fun).
